Introduction to Kunming

The Majestic Capital of Yunnan

Kunming, famously celebrated as the "City of Eternal Spring" for its exceptionally pleasant and temperate climate, is the political, economic, and cultural heart of Yunnan Province. With a history stretching back over two millennia, the city offers a profound cultural heritage interwoven with modern metropolitan vitality.

Surrounded by picturesque mountains and bordered by the expansive, shimmering waters of Dianchi Lake, Kunming boasts an unparalleled natural environment. It serves as a vital gateway to Southeast Asia, while being home to numerous distinct ethnic minorities, rendering it a spectacular melting pot of diverse cultures, exquisite cuisines, and captivating traditions.

Popular Destinations

Must-Visit Scenic Spots

The Stone Forest

A spectacular UNESCO World Heritage site located in Shilin County. It features a mesmerizing maze of towering, needle-like limestone pillars that closely resemble a massive petrified forest.

Explore More

Dianchi Lake

Widely honored as the "Pearl of the Plateau," this immense freshwater lake offers stunning sunset views and serves as a winter sanctuary for tens of thousands of beautiful red-billed seagulls.

Explore More

Green Lake Park

Cuihu Park is a tranquil urban oasis featuring emerald waters, blooming lotuses, and classical pavilions. It perfectly encapsulates the relaxed and leisurely lifestyle of Kunming residents.
